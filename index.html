Transform your body

With the proper training programme, use of different equipment and nutrition advices, transform your body within six months.

Barbell

Barbells are a valuable tool in the arsenal of a person who wants to gain strength efficiently and safely. Increasing the weight gradually over a period of time will tear down muscle tissue and then rebuild it, ultimately making you stronger. Because you add weight progressively with free weights, you don't have to add repetitions and lengthen your workout. Exercise experts also point to several other benefits of barbells.

Kettlebell

Kettlebells enchances functional strength, it promotes the entire body moving as a unit and that seems to apply very quickly to athletics. It's one of the reasons why so many people have adopted kettlebell training almost universally.

A non-glamorous moves like Turkish getup, which is one of the very best moves for jiu-jitsu, it's just phenomenal for your stability your core and your ability to get out from under the bottom positions. These principles of using the entire body with kettlebells, using different parts of your body, using your legs your core your ab all in one one workout.

Dumbell

Dumbells are excellent way for home workout, especially if you dont have time to go to the gym. They are great for building muscle mass, muscular endurance and promote weight loss. As oppose to the barbells, they require more effort in creating a stability during workout and can easily be stored in your home.

Calisthenics

Calisthenic exercises are the easiest way to get in shape because they require little to no equipment. With this kind of workout you will be focused on compound exercises, meaning you will perform exercises which involve a lot of different muscle groups.
